Gorkha Durbar
Info:
- the 16th-century Gorkha Durbar is a fort, palace and temple all in one
-The temple-palace perches high above Gorkha on a knife-edge ridge.
-with superb views over the Trisuli Valley and magnificent panoramas of the soaring mountains
-is the birthplace of Prithvi Narayan Shah.

Audience:
-who are interested in history of Nepal
-who is interested in trekking and natural sceneries
Age Range:
-suitable for tourists above 5 years
Route:
-104 km away from Kathmandu valley
-To reach the Durbar, you can climb an exhausting stairway of 1500 stone steps
