Swayambhunath Stupa
Info:
-an ancient religious architecture atop a hill in the Kathmandu Valley, west of Kathmandu city
-consists of a stupa, a variety of shrines and temples and a Tibetian monastery
-Much of Swayambhunath's iconography comes from the Vajrayana tradition of Newar Buddhism
Audience:
-People that want to know about Buddhist culture can visit it.
- People interested in complex architecture and structure can visit it.
Age Range:
-Tourists of all age range can visit this stupa.
Route:
It is inside Kathmandu Valley. So, it can be reached within an hour from parts of valley.
