Rara National Park
Info:
-is a protected area in the Himalayas of Nepal
-Its main feature is Rara Lake at an altitude of 2,990 m
-best times to visit the park are in September, October, April, and May
-One thousand and seventy species of flora are estimated from Rara National Park.
-Fifty-one species of mammals, 241 species of birds aare recorded to be here.
-is budding as a tourist destination.

Audience:
-who are interested in trekking
-who are interested in natural beauties and wildlife
-who are interested in photography
Age Range:
-suitable for adults
Route:
-can fly from Kathmandu to Nepalgunj, followed by a flight to Talcha
-from there, rara lake can be reached in a few hours by walking.
