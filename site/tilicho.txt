Tilicho Lake
Info:
-is a lake located in the Manang district of Nepal
-called the highest lake for its size in the world sometimes
-is the destination of one of the most popular side hikes of the Annapurna Circuit trek.
-was the site of one of the highest ever altitude scuba dives.
-Tilicho Lake is the ancient Kak Bhusundi Lake mentioned in the epic Ramayana.
Audience:
-who are interested in trekking
-who are interested in natural beauties
Age Range:
-above 21
Route:
-It is a part of Annapurna Circuit trek
-It takes a couple of days to reach here from Kathmandy valley.
