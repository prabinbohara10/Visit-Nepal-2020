Hanumandhoka Durbar Square
Info:
-is a complex of structures with the Royal Palace of the Malla kings
-gets its name from the stone image of Hanuman, the Hindu deity
-The Hanuman Dhoka Palace Museum is housed in the Hanuman Dhoka Palace 
-This section has artifacts relating to the lives of the various Shah kings
-There is also a section illustrating the repealing of the slave system.
Audience:
-who are interested in history of Nepal
-who is interested in old structured heritages.
who are interested in museums.
Age Range:
-suitable for all age range tourists
Route:
-4 km away from Kathmandu valley
