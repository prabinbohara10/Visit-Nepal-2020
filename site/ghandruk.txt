Ghandruk
Info:
- is a Village Development Committee in the Kaski District
-is readily accessible by public buses and private taxis from the provincial headquarter.
-is a common place for treks in the Annapurna range of Nepa
-The village hosts dramatic views of the mystic peaks 
-serves further as the gateway to the Poon hill trek
-has Gurung inhabitants who have their old lifestyle.
Audience:
-who is interested in cultures and traditions of Gurungs.
-who is interested in photography.

Age Range:
-suitable for tourists above 5
-who are fit to trek for few hours
Route:
-263 km away from Kathmandu valley.
-can be reached from Pokhara easily.
