Manakamana Temple
Info:
-situated in the Gorkha district of Nepal
-is the sacred place of the Hindu Goddess Bhagwati, an incarnation of Parvati.
- it is believed that Goddess Manakamana grants the wishes of all those who make the \npilgrimage to her shrine to worship her.
-The temple is located on a distinguished ridge 1,302 metres (4,272 ft) above sea level.
-The Manakamana temple is set in a square and looks across a massive sacred magnolia tree.
-It can be reached by cablecar without long treks.
Audience:
-Tourists who are interested to visit Hindu heritage.
-Tourists who like to travel places far away from city area.
