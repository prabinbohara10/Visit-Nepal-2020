Sagarmatha National Park
Info:
-is a national park in the Himalayas of eastern Nepal that is dominated by Mount Everest.
-the country's first national park that was inscribed as a Natural World Heritage Site
-The park contains the upper catchment areas of the Dudh Kosi river
-The forests provide habitat to at least 118 species of birds
-The temperature and available oxygen decrease with altitude
Audience:
-who are interested in wildlife
-who are interested in biodiversity
Age Range:
-suitable for adults
Route:
-approximately 135km from Kathmandu
