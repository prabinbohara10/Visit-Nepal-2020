Lumbini
Info:
-is a Buddhist pilgrimage site in the Rupandehi District of Province No. 5 in Nepal.
- Queen Mahamayadevi gave birth to Siddhartha Gautama in 563 BCE in Lumbini.
- It is one of many magnets for pilgrimage that sprang up in places pivotal to the life of the Buddha.
-The holy site of Lumbini is bordered by a large monastic zone.
Audience:
-Tourists who are interested about Buddhism can visit it.
-who are interested in structural buildings and stupas.
Age Range:
-Tourists of all age can visit it. 
-The trasnport to Lumbini is easy and everyone can access them.
Route:
-Lumbini is a 10-hour drive from Kathmandu and a 30-minute drive from Bhairahawa.
