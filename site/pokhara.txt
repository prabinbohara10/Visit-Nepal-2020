Pokhara
Info:

-one of the first choice by all tourists
-is the gateway to many treks
-has peaceful environment and amazing natural sceneries
-has Taal Barahi, Phewa lake, Begnas Lake.
-Lakeside is the major tourist attraction centre
Audience:
-who are interested in natural sceneries
-who are inerested in peaceful environment
-who are interested in photography
Age Range:
-suitable for all aged tourists
Route:
-196 km away from Kathmandu
-There is developed transportation inside this city
