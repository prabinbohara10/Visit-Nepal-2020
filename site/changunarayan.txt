Changunarayan Temple:
Info:
-It is one of the Hindu temples
-The temple is located in Changunarayan Municipality of Bhaktapur District, Nepal.
-This shrine is dedicated to lord Visnu and held in special reverence by the Hindu people.
-It remains a milestone in Nepali temple architecture with rich embossed works.
-There is an information centre at the entrance to Changu Village. They issue tickets to tourist.
-A private museum is located on the way to the temple.
-Followers of Hinduism and traditional artifacts can be seen during the visit.
Audience:
-Tourists interested in Hindu culture can visit it.
- Tourists interested in pagoda style heriages and buildings can visit it.
Age Range:
-Tourists of all age range can visit it.
