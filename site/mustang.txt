Mustang
Info:
- is an ancient forbidden kingdom sheltered by some of world's tallest peaks
-the second least populated district of Nepal
-Average elevation of Mustang is 13,200 ft (2,500m)
-has thousands of cliff dwellings, some highly inaccessible.
- is rich in trans-Himalayan biodiversity
-top tourist attractions are Lomanthang, the Mustangi royal palace, Tibetan art and culture.

Audience:
- who are interested in natural resources
-who are interested in traditions and caves
- who are interested in trekking

Age Range:
-suitable for all age
-not suitable for those having altitude sickness
Route:
-8074.8 miles away from Kathmandu
- has road facility from Kathmandu
