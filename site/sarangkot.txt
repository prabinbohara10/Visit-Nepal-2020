Sarangkot
Info:
-The village is located on Sarangkot mountain at an altitude of 1600m 
-is renowned for its panoramic Himalayan views Dhaulagiri, Annapurna and Manaslu
- is known for amazing views down on the city of Pokhara and Phewa lake.
-is a hotspot for paragliding.
Audience:
-who are interested in natual sceneries
-who are interested in paragliding
Age Range:
-adults are suitable to visit this place
Route:
-the distance from Kathmandu to Sarangkot is 215 km.
